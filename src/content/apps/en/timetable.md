Meet **Timetable**, the most intuitive timetable app that needs no manual.
Without complex typing, you can design your perfect weekly plan relying entirely on smooth touch gestures. It fully supports iPads as well as iPhones, and is currently being developed targeting both the iOS App Store and Android Play Store. It comes packed with various features to make your repetitive daily routine fresher and more convenient.

## ✨ Intuitive Experience
No need to painstakingly type out times. Just **drag across** the desired days and empty time slots to create a new schedule instantly.
- **Free Movement & Resizing**: Long press to select a schedule block, and move it to another day or time. Pull the top or bottom handles of the block to adjust its duration effortlessly in 15-minute increments.
- **Perfect Handling of Overlaps**: Multiple schedules at the same time? No problem! They overlap transparently so you can see them at a glance, and you can easily select and edit the exact one you want.
- **Lock for Safety**: Lock your completed timetable with the bottom padlock button to prevent accidental touches and edits.

## 🎨 Customizable to Your Taste
Adjust the default 6 AM - 10 PM range to match your lifestyle. If your weekends are free, you can hide Saturday and Sunday to utilize the screen space better.
A rich palette of pastel, neon, and earth tones is available to decorate each schedule beautifully and uniquely.

## 📲 Powerful Home Screen Widgets
Check your entire timetable right from the Home screen without opening the app.
- **Full Timetable Widget**: Display your entire weekly timetable in a grid to review the week ahead.
- **Today Widget**: Start your morning by checking today's schedule in a timeline format. A red line indicates the current time, making it clear what's happening right now.

## 🔔 Smart Notifications and Comparisons
- **Moments You Don't Want to Miss**: Set customized notifications at the start time, 5 minutes before, or 30 minutes before an event.
- **Overlay Timetables**: Overlay a friend's, colleague's, or another one of your own timetables semi-transparently. Spot free times or non-overlapping appointments instantly! Save it beautifully as an image or share it in a second via AirDrop.

## ☁️ Seamless Synchronization
Edit your timetable created on your iPhone directly from your iPad through iCloud sync. The iPad version specifically offers a pleasant editing environment taking full advantage of the large screen through Split View.
